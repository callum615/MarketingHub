# Content Strategy — galwayfinance.com.au

*Agreed 2026-07-12. Cadence: 1 post/week, drafted via Claude Code, staged as WP drafts by `wp-publish`, published by Callum after review.*

**Editorial calendar (source of truth):** Notion — the **Content Library** database on the Marketing Hub page (Galway Finance Hub teamspace), data source `40dcbf2b-102f-4efc-837e-86f426e6fe03`. Blog posts are the rows with `Format = Blog` / `Channel = Website`; social content lives in the same database. Blog fields: Pillar (blog pillar options), Target keyword, Buyer stage, Publish date, Live URL, Notes. Stage flow for blog posts: Idea (queued) → Drafting → Editing (= staged in WP awaiting Callum's review; Live URL set) → Published.

## Pillars

1. **Borrowing power & loan basics** (searchable backbone) — answers "how much can I borrow", deposits, LMI. Feeds /calculators/ and /home-loans-perth/.
2. **First home buying in WA** — grants/schemes (FHG, FHOG, stamp duty, Keystart), process guides. Feeds /first-home-buyer-loans-perth/. Builds on the existing FHG post.
3. **Construction & house-and-land** (differentiation niche) — progress payments, land-then-build timing, corridor estates. Few brokers cover this well; matches the northern-corridor ICP exactly. Feeds /house-and-land-construction-loans-perth/.
4. **Refinancing & loan health** — honest switching maths, timelines. Feeds /refinance-home-loan-perth/.
5. **Self-employed & non-standard income** — ABN income, add-backs, specialist lenders (panel advantage story). Feeds /commercial-finance-perth/.
6. **News/regulation** (opportunistic, not scheduled) — continue the newsjacking style of the two existing posts whenever rules change. These interrupt the queue, they don't wait for a slot.

## Rules of the pipeline

- Every post: compliance gate via `wp-publish` (disclaimer + CRN 579221 disclosure where credit products are promoted; no rates without comparison rate).
- Every post links to ≥1 service page + /booking/ CTA; posts within a pillar interlink.
- Voice per `.agents/product-marketing.md`: plain English, honest about trade-offs ("when it isn't worth it" angles build trust), no inflated claims.
- Existing posts live under /resources/ — keep that URL pattern.
- Blog posts alone can't prove ROI until GA4 gets a booking/lead conversion event (known gap, logged in memory).
